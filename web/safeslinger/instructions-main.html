After downloading the appropriate app for your Android device or iOS device, watch our walk through video or follow these written instructions to use SafeSlinger.

 
1. Enter the name you would like your friends to see, as well as a passphrase you would like to use to protect your private key.  First-time users will need to enter a passphrase twice for confirmation. Android mobile device screen shots

 
2. Selecting the Sling Keys tab will allow you to exchange public keys and other contact data.  See the exchange instructions page for details.

 
3. Selecting the Compose tab will allow you to select any recipient you have performed the above exchange with, and send a file and/or text message.

 
4. Selecting the Messages tab will allow you to review any messages sent or received. Long press on the message to download any attachments or remove the message.
