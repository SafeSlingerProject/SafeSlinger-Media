The SafeSlinger Software was built to allow you to verify the identity of those you communicate with to achieve better security while messaging. We aim to store only the minimum information necessary to deliver your private messages to their intended destination as quickly as possible, and remove information as soon as possible from our servers.

Passphrase Data
Your passphrase is never stored in any recoverable form, and as such, Carnegie Mellon as an operator of the SafeSlinger Software cannot help you recover your passphrase. Your only recovery option is to generate a new private key protected with a passphrase you choose.

Key Exchange Data
When you exchange keys ("Sling Keys") using SafeSlinger, your public key exchange data is encrypted with temporary keys only you and those you exchange with have access to. Carnegie Mellon does not have access to any temporary keys. Encrypted key exchange data is removed from SafeSlinger's server application after 10 minutes.

Message Data
When you send messages using SafeSlinger, your message data is encrypted with a private key only you have access to. Carnegie Mellon does not have access to any private keys. Your private key is protected with a passphrase chosen by you, which you should not share with anyone. Encrypted message data is removed from SafeSlinger's server application after 24 hours.

Push Notification Data
When you start using SafeSlinger, you register that your device may accept push notifications sent by other users via SafeSlinger's server application. Push notification content includes a random message id and may include encrypted message data to speed delivery if messages are small enough. These push notifications are used to prevent battery drain of your devices and are sent via push notification services operated by Apple and Google. Privacy policies for the respective services are found at http://www.apple.com/legal/privacy and http://www.google.com/policies/privacy.

Backup Data
Your encrypted private key and encrypted identity data can be backed up if you choose. All backup data may be removed from cloud backup services by uninstalling the application from your device. SafeSlinger uses cloud backup services operated by Apple and Google. Privacy policies for the respective services are found at http://www.apple.com/legal/privacy and http://www.google.com/policies/privacy.

Crash Report Data
If you encounter an error in the SafeSlinger Software and choose to send crash report data to its developers via email, text, or third-party such as Google Play and Crashlytics, Carnegie Mellon will retain that data for analysis. Crash report data can include: device id, model, versions, language, stack traces, and your comments to developers. SafeSlinger uses the crash reporting services of Crashlytics and Google Play to collect and store crash report data. Privacy policies for the respective services are found at http://try.crashlytics.com/terms and http://www.google.com/policies/privacy.

Log Data
SafeSlinger's server application automatically records specific log data which includes: application and delivery errors, IP address, time, and device type. All server log data is removed from SafeSlinger's server application after 7 days.

Information We CANNOT Keep
By nature of the security protocols implemented in the SafeSlinger Software, we cannot collect the following: your passphrase, your contact data, your address book data, your keys, your message contents or attachments.

Information We DO NOT Keep
To maintain your privacy we do not collect the following: your name, application settings, device id. We remove server log data after 7 days.

Information We DO Keep
We keep aggregate statistical data that includes: total number of messages sent daily by all users, total number of keys exchanged daily by all users, total number of unique users daily. We also keep crash data and feedback data for analysis and improvement of the SafeSlinger Software.

Changes and updates to this Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be revised periodically and this will be reflected by the "updated date" above. Please revisit this page to stay aware of any changes. Your continued use of the SafeSlinger Software constitutes your agreement to this Privacy Policy and any amendments.
